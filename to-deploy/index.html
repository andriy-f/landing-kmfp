Kirtan meditation is the answer for our times

In this world, there is no more powerful vibration than transcendental sound, and no more sublime method of meditation than Kirtan. Kirtan is sometimes called mantra music meditation, as it incorporates musical instruments singing, chanting, and dancing.
‍
Kirtan is an amazing experience. It brings light, love, and understanding, gives peace to our hearts, and influences all the living beings around us. In times when hate and conflict run rampant in our world, we can Become Peace through Kirtan Meditation.

About us

Kalindi and Yamuna are yoginis and the front women of the Atmasfera and Twin Rivers music bands from Ukraine. They created the Become Peace project and community, which aims to provide a platform to unite people together for meditations, events, and initiatives for peace. Become Peace is a community of people who support and promote the idea of ​​internal, personal peace, as well as peace in society.

What to expect at Kirtan?

At Kirtan, you can expect a concert with musicians, music, and Mantras instead of lyrics. Learning mantras can be much easier than learning the lyrics for every song. There will be plenty of wonderful people gathered together — including you! The musicians will play and sing and the whole room will be fulfilled with beautiful sounds.
Kirtan is something absolutely special — come and see! You’ll feel relaxed and comfortable right away.

What do you do during Kirtan?

Kirtan means congregational chanting — we all sing together, and you can join in or just listen. No vocal skills are needed — kirtan is a judgment-free zone! You can relax on your pillow (we’ll prepare it for you), or move to the music, and simply let the wonderful sound of mantras embrace you.

What Are Mantras?

Mantras are transcendental sound vibrations used in meditation since antiquity. Unlike silent meditation, mantra meditation involves hearing and repeating, making it easier to achieve focus. Mantra meditation can have powerful effects, such as relieving anxiety and stress, improving your relationships with others, and helping you become truly happy from within.
